1. From the “Edit” menu, choose “Find & Replace.” 
2. In the “Find” box, type “^.” (i.e., carat + period).
3. In the “Replace” box, type “&” (without the quotes, of course).
4. But you have to do one more thing for this to work.
5. At the bottom of the “Find & Replace” window you’ll see a button that says “More Options.”
6. Click on it, then select the box next to “Regular Expressions.”
7. You should now have a “Find & Replace” box that looks like this:
8. Now, hit “Replace All” and all of your apostrophes will disappear.
9. Your series of numbers will now be seen as numbers by your spreadsheet program and will be in cells formatted as number cells.
